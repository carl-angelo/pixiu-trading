Our Story

About Pixiu Trading

Pixiu Trading is a Filipino-owned business located in Tayabas, Quezon, officially launched on July 28, 2023. Founded by a former member of the Philippine National Police and later the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas, our founder followed his entrepreneurial passion to build a business rooted in service and quality.

We specialize in thermal paper rolls, office supplies, sports equipment, and customized items — practical solutions that streamline and support your everyday business operations.

We are proud partners with JJIM Trading, a trusted supplier of office equipment and business essentials to LGU offices across the region. Together, we deliver quality and consistency to every client we serve.
