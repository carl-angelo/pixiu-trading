About Us


Pixiu Trading is a Filipino-owned business located in Tayabas, Quezon, officially launched on July 28, 2023. It was founded by a former member of the Philippine National Police who entered service on June 16, 2014. Seeking greater purpose, he transferred to the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas, but later realized his passion lay in entrepreneurship. This led him to fully resign from public service to establish his own business.


Pixiu Trading's vision is to provide dependable and high-quality products tailored to the daily needs of businesses. We specialize in thermal papers, custom-printed umbrellas, and various equipment essential for business operations, offering practical solutions that help streamline and support your workflow.
At Pixiu Trading, we believe that reliable supplies form the backbone of efficient workspaces. Whether you're a local entrepreneur or a government agency, we are committed to delivering fast, affordable, and flexible service that adapts to your requirements.


We are proud partners with JJIM Trading, a trusted supplier of office equipment and business essentials to LGU offices across the region. Together, we strengthen our reach and resources, ensuring we serve you better with quality and consistency.


Let Pixiu Trading be your go-to partner for your everyday business needs — dependable, ready, and here to grow with you.
